Site Plan - Guevara Auto Service

1. Site Name

Guevara Auto Service

2. Purpose

Provide local customers in Bergenfield, NJ with clear information about auto repair and maintenance services, allow them to explore offerings, and make it easy to contact or schedule an appointment.

3. Target Audience

  • Local car owners in Bergenfield, NJ
  • Customers looking for trustworthy and affordable car maintenance and repairs
  • Individuals seeking to book appointments or learn more about available services

4. Goals

  • Showcase featured and full list of services
  • Provide an easy way to contact the shop or book appointments
  • Build trust with professional design and accurate business information
  • Ensure mobile-friendly and accessible browsing
